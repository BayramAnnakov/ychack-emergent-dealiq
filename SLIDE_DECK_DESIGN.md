## DELIVERY TIPS FOR THE AA QUOTE

### Vocal Technique
1. **Lead with curiosity**: "I once asked an American Airlines VP..."
2. **Quote with slight pause**: "His answer? [2-beat pause] 'We can't compete...'"
3. **Let it land**: After the quote, pause 1 second. Eye contact with judges.
4. **Bridge to solution**: "This isn't just AA's problem... [continue]"

### Body Language
- **During quote**: Stand still, serious expression
- **After quote**: Slight head nod (acknowledging the pain)
- **Transition**: Open hand gesture toward screen, energy shifts up

### Why This Works
The AA quote does three things:
1. **Establishes your credibility** - You've talked to Fortune 500 VPs
2. **Validates the market** - If AA has this problem, it's huge
3. **Creates urgency** - The talent war is unsustainable

Then you swoop in with: "What if AI could do both jobs?" â†’ Perfect setup for DealIQ.
